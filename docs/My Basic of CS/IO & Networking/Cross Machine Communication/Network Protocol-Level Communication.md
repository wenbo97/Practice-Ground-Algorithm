> Communication mechanisms at the network protocol level for cross-machine.

1. **Raw Sockets**
	- `TcpClient`, `UdpClient`, `Socket`
	- *Positioning* - The cornerstone of the Internet of Everything, understanding the core of TCP packet fragmentation and ressembly.

2. **RPC Frameworks**
	- `gPRC (based on Protobuf and HTTP/2)`
	- *Positioning* - The modern standard for microservice communication, like a "strongly typed pipe".

3. **Message Queues**
	- `RabbitMQ`, `Kafka`
	- *Positioning* - Asynchronous decoupling.
